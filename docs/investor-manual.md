# Investor Manual — How to Use GoldTrade

This guide walks you through everything you can do as a GoldTrade member, from
opening an account to withdrawing your profit.

**In short:** deposit via Binance USDT → confirm your receipt on WhatsApp → once
approved, earn 1% of your principal every day → invite friends to earn 5% of their
deposits → withdraw whenever you like.

---

## 1. Open an account

1. Go to **[goldtrade.markets](https://goldtrade.markets)** and click **Open an Account**
   (or go straight to `/join`).
2. Fill in:
   - **Full Name**
   - **Email Address**
   - **Phone Number**
   - **Password** (and confirm it)
3. Tick **I agree to the Terms and Conditions**.
4. Click **Open an Account**.

If you arrived through a friend's referral link, you'll see a **"Referred by …"**
badge at the top of the form — that means your account will be linked to them and
they'll earn a bonus on your future deposits. (You don't have to do anything extra.)

### Verify your email

After signing up you'll receive a **verification email** from GoldTrade. Click the
link inside it to confirm your address. **You can't log in until your email is
verified.** Clicking the link also activates your membership.

> Didn't get the email? You'll be shown a "verification pending" screen with an
> option to resend it. Check your spam folder too.

### Your GoldTrade ID

Once you're in, you're assigned a permanent **GoldTrade ID** like `GT-00007`. You'll
find it on your **Settings** page and in the account menu (top‑right avatar). Use it
whenever you need to identify yourself to support.

## 2. Log in

1. Go to **`/login`**.
2. Enter your email and password. (Use the 👁 icon to reveal your password if needed.)
3. Click **Log In**. You'll see a brief **"Signing you in…"** screen and then land
   on your dashboard.

Forgot your password? Click **Forgot password?**, enter your email, and follow the
reset link we send you.

---

## 3. Your dashboard

The dashboard (`/investor/dashboard`) is your home screen. It shows:

- **Total Portfolio Value** — your current cash balance.
- **Cash Balance** — money you can withdraw right now.
- **Principal (earns 1% daily)** — the deposited amount your daily profit is
  calculated from.
- **Gold Rate · 24K / Tola (USD)** — a live chart of the gold price, set by the
  GoldTrade team, with the 30‑day trend.
- **Quick Actions** — shortcuts to Deposit and Withdraw.
- **Recent Transactions** — every deposit, daily‑profit credit, referral bonus, and
  withdrawal, newest first.

The left sidebar (or the ☰ menu on mobile) links to Dashboard, Withdraw, Deposit,
Referral, and Settings. The 🔔 bell (top‑right) shows your notifications.

## 4. Make a deposit

Deposits are made with **Binance USDT** and confirmed by our team over WhatsApp.

### Step 1 — Pay

1. Go to **Deposit** and click **Deposit Funds**.
2. You'll see our **Binance USDT** details:
   - **Network** (e.g. TRX / TRC20)
   - **Address** — use the 📋 copy button to copy it exactly.
3. Send your USDT to that address from your own wallet.

### Step 2 — Confirm your receipt

1. Click **Next**.
2. You'll see our **WhatsApp number** (copy it with the 📋 button).
3. **Send a screenshot of your payment receipt to that WhatsApp number.** This is
   how our team matches your payment to your account.
4. Optionally, paste your **Transaction ID / Reference** (e.g. the Binance TXID)
   into the field.
5. Click **Submit Deposit Request**.

Your request now appears in **Deposit History** with the status **PENDING**.

### Step 3 — Wait for approval

Our team verifies your receipt (usually within 24 hours), enters the exact amount
they confirmed, and approves it. When they do:

- The amount is added to **both** your cash balance and your principal balance.
- Your principal now starts earning **1% every day**.
- If someone referred you, they receive their 5% bonus automatically.
- You get a notification: *"Your deposit of $X was approved."*

If something's wrong with a receipt, a request may be **REJECTED** instead — you'll
be notified and can submit a new one.

> **Note:** You never type the deposit amount yourself. The admin sets it from your
> actual receipt, so the booked amount always matches what you really sent.

## 5. Earn 1% daily profit

Once you have an approved deposit (a non‑zero **principal balance**), you
automatically earn **1% of that principal, every single day**, added to your **cash
balance**.

- It's **simple interest, not compounding** — the 1% is always calculated from your
  principal, which only grows when you make another approved deposit.
- Example: with **$1,000** principal, you earn **$10 per day**, every day, credited
  to your cash balance. After 10 days you'd have **$100** in profit — and your
  principal is still $1,000, still earning $10/day.
- Profit is credited automatically once per day; you'll see a **"Daily 1% profit on
  principal"** row in your transaction history.

## 6. Refer friends and earn 5%

Go to **Referral** (`/investor/refer`) to find:

- **Your Referral Code** (e.g. `GOLD-ABCD1234`) — copy it with the 📋 button.
- **Your Invite Link** (e.g. `https://goldtrade.markets/join?ref=GOLD-ABCD1234`) —
  share this so friends are auto‑linked to you when they sign up.
- **Total Earned** — your lifetime referral commission.
- **Investors Referred** — how many people joined through you.
- **Referral Earnings** — a breakdown of each friend's deposit and your commission.

**How it pays out:** every time someone you referred makes a deposit that an admin
**approves**, you're credited **5% of that deposit** to your cash balance —
automatically, with no limit on how many friends you refer.

> Referral bonuses land in your **cash balance** (spendable/withdrawable). They do
> **not** increase your principal, so they don't compound into daily profit — they're
> a straight cash reward.

## 7. Withdraw your money

Go to **Withdraw** (`/investor/withdraw`). At the top you'll see your **Available
Cash**, **Pending** withdrawals, and **Total Withdrawn** to date.

1. Click **Request a Withdrawal**.
2. Enter the **Amount (USD)** — it must be within your available cash balance.
3. Choose a **Payout Method**:
   - **JazzCash** — enter your JazzCash number.
   - **Binance USDT** — enter your Binance (TRX network) address.
4. Click **Submit Request**.

Your request appears in **Withdrawal History** as **PENDING**. An admin reviews it
and either **approves** (funds are sent to the details you provided and deducted from
your cash balance) or **rejects** it. Withdrawals typically settle within 1–2
business days.

## 8. Notifications

The 🔔 bell at the top‑right shows important events: deposit approvals, referral
bonuses, and account credits. A gold dot means you have unread notifications.

- Open the bell to read them (opening marks them read).
- Click **Clear all** to remove them (they disappear instantly).
- Click the **✕** to close the panel.

## Frequently asked questions

**When exactly is my daily profit paid?**
Once per calendar day (at midnight, server time). You'll never be credited twice in
the same day.

**Do referral bonuses and daily profit earn their own daily profit?**
No. Only your **principal** (approved deposits) earns the 1% daily. Bonuses and
profit go to your spendable cash balance.

**Why do I have to message WhatsApp?**
Payments are verified by a human so every deposit is matched to a real receipt. It's
what keeps the ledger accurate.

**What's a "tola"?**
A traditional unit of gold weight used in South Asia (~11.66 g). The dashboard shows
the 24K gold rate in **USD per tola**.

**I sent money but my balance is still $0.**
Your deposit is probably still **PENDING**. Make sure you (1) submitted the deposit
request in the app and (2) sent your receipt screenshot to our WhatsApp number.
Approval usually happens within 24 hours.
